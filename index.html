About

SEAIS brings leading researchers in software engineering and AI together with experienced practitioners and ambitious students to reflect on the state of the art in (human-centered) software engineering and AI, and to co-shape its future.

The symposium is held with the generous support of the Hasso Plattner Institute and its Chair for Software Engineering and AI.
